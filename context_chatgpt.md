# External Context: AI IT Strategy 2026

## 1. Research Foundation

- Up to **70% of tasks are theoretically automatable**
- Only **10–35% currently realized in practice**
- This defines the **“Integration Era”**

### Key Concept: Exposure Gap
- Difference between:
  - **Theoretical Exposure** (what AI can do)
  - **Observed Exposure** (what organizations actually do)
- Represents:
  - Organizational friction
  - Skill gaps
  - Process limitations

**Strategic Insight:**
This gap is a **window for competitive advantage**

## 3. Operational Evolutions

### 30% Mandate
- At least **30% of critical logic must be manually created/refactored by humans**
- Prevents:
  - Black-box dependency
  - Knowledge erosion
- Ensures:
  - Explainability
  - Auditability

---

### Evolution of Global Delivery Strategy
- Shift from labor arbitrage to capability specialization

New model:
- Onshore → Context Engineering leadership
- Global hubs → AI-Ops & Audit Centers

---

### The Junior Gap
- Entry-level roles shrink significantly
- New role:
  - **AI-Audit Associate**
    - Oversees AI outputs
    - Learns through validation
