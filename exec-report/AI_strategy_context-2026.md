# CONTEXT & KNOWLEDGE BASE: AI IT STRATEGY 2026

## 1. Research Foundation
- Anthropic Research (2026): Identifies 'Observed Exposure' vs 'Theoretical Exposure'. 70% of tasks can be automated, but only ~10-35% are currently integrated due to friction. This is the 'Integration Era'.
	- https://www.anthropic.com/research/labor-market-impacts
- Josh Kale Exposure Map: Shows that highly educated, high-earning digital roles (IT/Legal/Finance) are the most exposed. Exposure is a function of digital proximity.
	- https://github.com/JoshKale/jobs

## 2. Core Strategic Concepts
- The Exposure Gap: The window between what AI can do and what we are doing. Bridging this is an upskilling opportunity.
- Spec-Driven Development: Shifting the source of truth from code syntax to high-fidelity specifications.
- Context Engineering: The primary new discipline for technologists; defining business intent, constraints, and logic so accurately that AI execution is seamless.
- Vanishing Boundaries: The melting of the wall between Business Experts and Technologists; IT shifts to being a Platform and Guardrail provider.

## 3. Operational Evolutions
- 30% Mandate: To prevent 'Black Box' systems, humans must manually design/refactor 30% of critical logic to retain tribal knowledge.
- Global Delivery Strategy: Shift offshore from 'bulk labor' to 'Specialized Audit & AI-Ops Centers'. Onshore moves to 'Lead Context Engineering'.
- The Junior Gap: Mitigating the skill void by redefining Juniors as 'AI-Audit Associates' who learn by overseeing AI output.

## 4. Strategic Dividends
- Legacy Liquidation: Using AI to refactor legacy debt (The Great Refactoring).
- Unit Cost of Intelligence: Financial shift from OpEx Labor (Headcount) to Compute/Token costs.
- Autonomous DevSecOps: Security generated and tested at the speed of code.

## 5. Role Transformation Matrix
- Software Engineer -> Logic & Security Auditor
- Business Analyst -> Context Architect
- QA/Tester -> Guardrail Engineer
- Database Admin -> Knowledge Engineer
- Project Manager -> Agent Orchestrator
- Junior Engineer -> AI-Audit Associate

## 6. Implementation Roadmap
- Phase 1: Internal Exposure Map (Audit roles).
- Phase 2: Usage Telemetry Audit (Analyze tool adoption like O365/GitHub Copilot).
- Phase 3: Legacy Audit for refactoring pilots.
